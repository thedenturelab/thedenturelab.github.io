About

Summary

We are a small team of three very experienced dental technicians working from a laboratory in Gillingham, Kent. We offer many services to both dentists and the general public ranging from crown repairs to full dentures. A full list of our services can be found here.

Our Technicians

Chris qualified in 1996 as a Dental Technician at Lambeth College where he was awarded a BTEC National Diploma in Dental Technology. He has completed a Clinical Dental Technology Degree at Preston University, UCLAN in 2019. Chris is registered with the General Dental Council (Number 141679) and is a member of the British Association of Clinical Dental Technicians.
Since 1991, Chris has been highly experienced in supplying dentures to his patients in a friendly, caring and professional manner. His advanced knowledge helps him solve even the most challenging of denture problems and offers that much needed reassurance that the dentures he creates, will more than meet with his patient's expectations. Chris has also furthered his skill and knowledge by doing numerous courses including the Better Dentures course.

Nick Qualified in 1999 as a Dental Technician at Lambeth College where he was awarded a BTEC National Diploma in Dental Technology and is registered with the General Dental Council (Number 141147). Nick has been making dentures since 1994, serving a 5-year apprenticeship at a busy Dental Practice, which allowed him access to seeing patients in the clinical setting. He is also committed to furthering his knowledge on dentures and has completed the Better Dentures course.

Iain Qualified in 1987 as a Dental Technician at Lambeth College where he was awarded a BTEC National Diploma in Dental Technology. He is registered with the General Dental Council (Number 141171). Iain has been making dentures since 1982, serving a 5-year apprenticeship at one of the busiest Dental Practices in the Medway towns, working for many years in the surgery alongside dentists and surgeons. Iain is also fully trained as a Master Ceramist and has completed many courses including Better Dentures.
